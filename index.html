Santa Claus

The man, the myth, the legend, Santa Claus has been working for a long time, and somehow is still going strong. Hopefully he doesn't burn out anytime soon!

Mrs. Claus

Mrs. Claus is a lot more reserved than her jolly husband. She prefers to be hidden from the spotlight, and would rather read a book or bake than converse with strangers. She is particularly fond of the reindeer.

The Crew

The workers behind all the magic, the elves almost never catch a break. They don't seem to mind, however, as none have ever been seen complaining about their outlandish work hours. All elves love doing for others.

Favorite Songs

  • Santa Claus is
    Coming to Town
  • Rockin' Around the
    Christmas Tree
  • O Holy Night

An elf-favorite for many years, Santa Claus is Coming to Town reigns as the number one classic Christmas song in the North Pole. If you happen to visit Santa's Village, you'll often hear this jolly tune coming from homes throughout the neighborhoods, regardless of the time of year.

Rockin' Around the Christmas Tree is another Christmas song loved by the members of Santa's village, particularly Rudolph. He's hummed the chorus so many times that his fellow reindeer have to skip the song whenever it comes on, or just leave the room itself.

This song takes a bit more of a serious tone than the other two, being a far older song. It was first composed in 1847 in France. Santa's elves revere this song, and often when it comes on, everyone stops what they're doing to join into the song, or just to listen along.

About Us

We are a company whose highest objective is to provide toys for children every Christmas Eve. We have been in business for thousands of years now. Our elves work extraordinarily hard every day of the year to make sure that every child gets what they'd like.
